Advanced Silence Techniques
Once you've mastered basic presence, you can begin working with silence as a more sophisticated tool:
The Strategic Pause: In conversations, after someone asks you a question, pause for three full seconds before respond- ing. Not because you don't know the answer, but because your response deserves that much consideration. Watch how this simple delay changes the entire dynamic of the interaction.
The Comfortable Cut-Off: When someone is talking at you rather than with you — dominating the conversation, lecturing, or clearly just waiting for their turn to speak — simply stop engaging. Don't announce you're done. Don't explain why you're no longer participating. Just gently withdraw your atten- tion and let them continue their monologue to an audience
